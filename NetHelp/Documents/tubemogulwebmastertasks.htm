TubeMogul Webmaster Tasks

To submit the video feed to TubeMogul

1.  Copy the feed URL you obtained after you added the feed. See KMC Publisher Tasks for TubeMogul Syndication.

2.  Login to your Kaltura–TubeMogul account.

3.  Go to Advanced Tools, select More Tools, and then select MRSS Feed Manager.

4.  Submit your feed.
