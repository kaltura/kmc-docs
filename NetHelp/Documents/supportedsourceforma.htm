Supported Source Formats

The following source formats are supported and may be ingested by the Kaltura platform:

FLV/F4V, MOV/MP4/QT/M4V, AVI, 3GP, ASF/WMV, MPG/M1V/M2V/MPEG-TS, MKV, Ogg, RM, WEBM

Additional formats may be supported as well but have not been verified.
